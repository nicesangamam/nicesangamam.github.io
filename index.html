About Us

NICE SANGAMAM Charitable Trust was established on April 28, 2025, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, by 1995-99 batch of Noorul Islam College of Engineering students, with a primary objective: "To help the poor people for Education & Health."

Our vision is to create a society where everyone has access to fundamental necessities for a dignified life. We aim to achieve this by actively seeking and utilizing funds, donations, and resources to support various initiatives across India, focusing on sustainable development and empowerment.

What We Do

Learn More What We Do And Get Involved

Education

We provide assistance and scholarships to deserving students, helping them pursue their academic dreams and break the cycle of poverty.

Healthcare

We offer emergency medical aid and subsidize essential treatments for underprivileged individuals, ensuring access to critical care for all.

Social Upliftment

We empower communities by providing financial aid for essentials to help families build stability and self-reliance.
